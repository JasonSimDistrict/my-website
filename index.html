Frequently Asked

The questions buyers ask first

Quick answers to the things people enquire about most. Need more depth? Drop a message.

Is ProjectHome.sg free to use?+
Yes. The full 7-step buying guide, all blog posts, and market reports are free to read. WhatsApp enquiries are also free — no obligation to engage Jason as your agent.
Do you cover HDB and ECs, or only private property?+
All three. The roadmap is property-class agnostic — and we publish dedicated guides on BTO vs. resale, EC eligibility, decoupling for couples, and CCR/RCR/OCR private launches.
How quickly does Jason respond on WhatsApp?+
Within one business day for sure — usually within a few hours during Singapore working hours. Late nights and weekends, you'll get a reply the next morning.
Is Jason an independent advisor?+
Jason is a District Director at Huttons Asia, one of Singapore's largest private real estate agencies. The content here is independent of any specific developer's marketing — opinions on launches, districts, and pricing are his own.
I'm not buying for another year. Is it too early to reach out?+
Not at all — early is better. The buyers who get the best outcomes typically start 12–18 months before purchase, so they can watch the market, refine their criteria, and act when the right opportunity appears.
