### What is PrimeKey Analysis?

PrimeKey Analysis is a proprietary scoring system that evaluates a property across 8 critical drivers to calculate its ultimate "Investability Score". Instead of relying on a gut feeling or shiny showroom floor tiles, PrimeKey evaluates verified data points to generate an undeniable objective score from 0 to 100%. 

The primary goal of PrimeKey Analysis is simple: **protect the downside**. By ensuring an asset is "hard to fail" and classifying it as Investment Grade, the framework minimizes your risk, allowing the upside of the investment to naturally take care of itself.

### The 8 Pillars of a Smart Investment

PrimeKey Analysis evaluates properties based on 8 proven growth factors that actually matter to a property's future performance:

1. **MRT Connectivity:** Walking distance to train stations (especially under 5 minutes) directly impacts rentability and livability, widening your pool of future buyers. 
2. **Growth Hotspots:** Properties located within or near URA Master Plan transformation zones have historically delivered significantly higher capital appreciation.
3. **Government Land Sales (GLS):** Future land releases nearby act as a catalyst. As land prices escalate over time, they naturally lift the asset value of earlier launches.
4. **Project Size:** Larger developments (400+ units) enjoy better economies of scale for maintenance, more facilities, and higher transaction volumes to support future bank valuations.
5. **Remaining Tenure:** A healthy remaining lease (over 90 years) acts as a defensive play, preventing equity decay and securing your exit strategy before the property stagnates.
6. **Rental Yield:** Higher rental yields act as the "Cashflow King," providing a safety net and strong holding power during market fluctuations.
7. **Nearby Primary Schools:** The "Parent Premium" guarantees consistent demand. Being within 1km of top-tier primary schools provides a permanent, recession-proof pool of buyers.
8. **Upgrader Demand (MOP Clusters):** High volumes of nearby HDB flats reaching their Minimum Occupation Period (MOP) create natural, localized demand from homeowners ready to upgrade.
