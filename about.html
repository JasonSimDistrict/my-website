Why ProjectHome.sg exists

Most Singaporean buyers I meet describe the same problem: too much information, too little clarity. Property listings, agent calls, market reports, social media gurus, government cooling-measure announcements — all coming at once, often contradicting each other. The decisions that follow are some of the largest financial commitments a household will ever make.

ProjectHome.sg is my answer to that. It's structured, honest, and free. The 7-step buyer roadmap, the quarterly market reports, the new launch reviews — they're built so that even if we never meet, you can move with confidence.

How I work

My approach with private clients is consultative, not transactional. That means:

  • I start with your why. Whether you're buying a home for the next decade or building a portfolio for the next thirty years changes everything that follows.
  • Data over opinions. I'll show you the URA caveats, the 5-year and 10-year PSF trends, and the unsold-inventory picture — not just my view.
  • Long-term lens. Every recommendation considers your exit, even if you plan to stay forever. Optionality is value.
  • No pressure. The right property at the wrong moment is still the wrong property. If the answer is "wait", I'll tell you.

Areas of focus

I cover the full Singapore residential spectrum — HDB resale and BTO, Executive Condos, and private property across CCR, RCR, and OCR. My specific areas of focus include:

  • First-time HDB and EC buyers planning their long-term path
  • HDB upgraders moving into private property
  • Decoupling and second-property strategy for married couples
  • New launch evaluation and unit selection
  • Investment-grade district and project analysis
