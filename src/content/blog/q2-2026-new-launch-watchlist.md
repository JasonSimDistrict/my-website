## What we'll be watching at preview

For each project, three questions:

- **Is the launch PSF defensible against 5-year resale comps within 800 m?**
- **What's the unit mix?** Heavy 1-bedder skew = investor product, heavier 3-4 bedder skew = owner-occupier product. Resale liquidity diverges sharply between these two.
- **Are the stacks priced to move?** If the developer is leaving money on premium stacks, the project will struggle in months 3–6.
